Grace Walker is a junior at the University of Maryland double majoring in Multiplatform Journalism and Criminology and Criminal Justice. She has worked for over two years as the Social Media Intern for Friends & Fiction, a weekly web show hosted by four New York Times bestselling authors, curating their Instagram account, @friendsandfiction. She has an interest in social media content creation and audience engagement work as well as broadcast production and crime writing. She was born in New Jersey and raised on the Jersey Shore.
